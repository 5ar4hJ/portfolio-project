About me

Sarah Ngunangwa

SJ's career spans clinical practice, food service innovation, and academia. She gathers enriching recipes that enchant from a myriad of heritages, ingeniously refining them with cost-effective yet nutrient-packed alternatives wherever her adventures lead. She employs a blend of evidence-based knowledge and practical insight, with emphasis on health equity; embodying a dedication to ensuring equitable access to resources for healthier living. In her spare moments, she delights in reading, painting, and exploring various cultures, driven by a profound curiosity about human behavior and the enigmas of the mind.
